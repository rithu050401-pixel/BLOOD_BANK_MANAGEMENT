BLOOD DONATION IN 4 EASY STEPS

1. REGISTER

Fill in basic personal details and health history so we can prepare for your donation.

2. MINI-PHYSICAL AND INTERVIEW

A quick health check (blood pressure, hemoglobin) and a brief interview to confirm eligibility.

3. DONATION

Typical donation takes about 8–10 minutes. Our staff will ensure your comfort and safety.

4. RELAX

Enjoy refreshments and rest for a few minutes — stay hydrated and follow post-donation advice.
