About Blood Bank Directory

Helping you save lives by providing quick access to blood bank locations and availability.

Our Mission

The mission of this Blood Bank Directory is to make blood donations and emergency support more accessible. By providing detailed information on blood banks in Chennai, we aim to reduce delays during medical emergencies and ensure patients receive timely help.

Our Vision

To create a platform that connects blood donors, hospitals, and patients in need, making life-saving resources available to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

Key Features

  • Browse a list of 20 major blood bank centers in Chennai.
  • Check real-time blood availability (your own updated data).
  • Quick access to Google Maps for navigation.
  • Contact details and 24/7 availability of blood banks.
  • User-friendly interface compatible with mobile devices.

How to Use This Website

  • Click on any blood bank to view detailed blood group availability.
  • Use the "View location" button to open Google Maps for directions.
  • Call the blood bank using the provided phone numbers in emergencies.
  • Check multiple centers to find the nearest blood with sufficient units.
